# 🚀 Rozgaar Mitra AI ## Build for Good 2026 | ROZGAAR Track ### Empowering India's Informal Workforce through AI Rozgaar Mitra AI is an AI-powered Voice-First Employment Platform designed for daily wage workers, gig workers, domestic helpers, farmers, and other informal workers. The platform removes literacy and digital barriers by allowing workers to use simple voice messages in their own language. --- ## Problem Millions of workers struggle because: - No digital profile - No easy job discovery - Language barriers - Low digital literacy - No trusted work history --- ## Our Solution Rozgaar Mitra AI helps workers by providing: - 🎤 Voice Registration - 📍 Nearby Job Matching - 🤖 AI Skill Suggestions - 📒 Voice Attendance Diary - 👥 Group Job Formation - 🆘 Women's Voice SOS - 📶 Offline SMS Support --- ## Target Users - Daily Wage Workers - Construction Workers - Electricians - Plumbers - Farmers - Domestic Helpers - Women Workers --- ## Social Impact - Increase employment - Improve income - Promote digital inclusion - Support women safety - Enable skill development --- ## Build for Good 2026 Track: ROZGAAR Created by: Mohit Dhaarse
